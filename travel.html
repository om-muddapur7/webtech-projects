Explore the rich history, iconic landmarks, and indulge in delicious French cuisine. Your adventure awaits in the heart of Europe.

Eiffel Tower

One of the most recognizable landmarks in the world, the Eiffel Tower offers stunning views of Paris from its observation decks.

The Basilique du Sacré-Coeur de Montmartre

The Montmartre basilica boasts an all-white façade, Romano-Byzantine design, and mosaic ceiling unlike any other religious structure in Paris.

Louvre Museum

The Louvre is the world's largest and most visited art museum, home to masterpieces like the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo.

Notre Dame Cathedral

A masterpiece of Gothic architecture, the Notre Dame Cathedral is an iconic religious landmark with breathtaking stained glass windows.
